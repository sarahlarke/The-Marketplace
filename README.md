# The-Marketplace# The Marketplace

**Everyone Has Skills**

A polished Streamlit demo showing how Westminster could unlock hidden talent, surface passion skills, connect colleagues to short-term opportunities, and build a live picture of workforce capability over time.

---

## What this demo shows

The Marketplace is designed to help the organisation:

- make hidden skills visible
- connect people to real, short-term opportunities
- help colleagues practise passion skills in a value-adding environment
- build trust through endorsements
- generate insight into workforce capability
- support better outcomes for residents
- align to Fit for the Future ambitions

---

## Core features

### Skills Passports
A living profile for each colleague, including:

- core skills
- hidden skills not used in their day job
- passion skills they would like to practise more
- interests
- endorsements
- completed contributions

### Opportunities
Teams can post short-term opportunities or requests for help, including:

- support needed
- skills required
- tags
- time commitment
- resident impact
- Fit for the Future link

### Smart Matching and AI Nudges
The app suggests colleagues who may be a strong fit based on:

- skills match
- hidden skills
- passion skills
- interests
- availability
- preferred commitment
- endorsements

### Endorsements
Colleagues can recognise one another’s contributions, helping to build:

- trust
- visibility
- confidence
- recognition based on real contribution, not just role title

### Insights
The app provides a simple insight layer showing:

- recorded skills across the workforce
- hidden and passion skill potential
- contribution patterns
- endorsement trends
- directorate spread
- capability visibility over time

---

## Why it matters

Across organisations, colleagues often have valuable skills, passions and interests that sit outside their day-to-day role. These can remain hidden simply because there is no easy way to see or access them.

This prototype shows how a Marketplace could help unlock that untapped and aspiring talent while creating value for teams and better outcomes for residents.

It also demonstrates how the concept could support a more agile, skilled, connected and insight-led workforce.
